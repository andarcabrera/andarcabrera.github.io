I am a Romanian transplant in Chicago, via the East Coast. The Windy City offered me the best of both worlds, a dynamic and cosmopolitan city, and, at the same time, a big Romanian community that made me feel I have a home away from home.

Now that I caught roots in this fine city, the restless side of me set its eyes on new adventures, this time in my professional life. I always had a knack for computers and was usually the go to person for IT troubleshooting in my former jobs.

The timing couldn't be more perfect when I learned about DEVbootcamp and here I am a developer in training ready to enter this new field that has proved very exciting and rewarding so far.

If you'd like to see a sample of my work you can checkout my projects. My blogs will give you an idea of what I'm learning and thinking about this experience, or you can always contact me.
